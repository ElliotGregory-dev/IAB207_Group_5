Mexican Mayhem


Event Date:

20/10/2022 - 30/10/2022

Event Time:

6:00pm - 11:59pm

Event Location:

Little Stanley St, South Brisbane QLD 4101

Description:

Mexican Mayhem is the absolute one stop shop for all your Mexican food needs! Have you been looking for that perfect churro? Do you desire to taste some of the spiciest
foods in the world? Or perhaps you've never tried any type of Mexican dish before? Well don't be shy and come on down! Perfect for families to bring with the little ones! This
year we bring a whole range on new vendors, as well as some favourites from last year. Carlito's Churro's are making a long awaited return as well as Guzman y Gomez.
We'd also like to welcome one of our latest vendors, Juan Pablo's Bonanza, specialising in all things traditional!
